The Origin Story

Two Paths. One Broken Industry. A Better Way.

Kasim's Story

Kasim Aslam spent 15 years and dozens of EA hires learning exactly what makes executive assistants fail. As CEO of Solutions 8 — the #1 Google Ads agency worldwide — he experienced every failure mode: EAs who waited for instructions, EAs who couldn't handle ambiguity, EAs who created more work than they saved. He realized the problem was never the person. It was the system — or the lack of one.

Ivan's Story

Ivan Bunin started as an Executive Assistant at 18 with no resources and no playbook. Through relentless learning and proactive ownership, he grew from EA to CTO at Solutions 8, helping scale the company to 200+ clients and an 8-figure acquisition. He knows what a Right Hand can become — because he was one. He also knows exactly what it takes, because he lived every step of that transformation.

The Realization

After the exit, Kasim and Ivan looked at the EA industry and saw a broken model: agencies that placed warm bodies without training, without systems, without any investment in the founder's ability to delegate. They knew both sides — what a founder needs and what an EA needs to succeed. So they built the only program that serves both.
