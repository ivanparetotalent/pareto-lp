The Science of Matching

Why Predictive Index Changes Everything

Most agencies match on skills and availability. We match on behavioral DNA — the invisible layer that determines whether a founder and EA actually click.

What PI Measures

Predictive Index maps four behavioral drives: Dominance (assertiveness vs. accommodation), Extraversion (social energy), Patience (pace of work), and Formality (structure vs. flexibility). These aren't personality tests — they're predictive models of how someone will naturally behave under pressure.

Why It Matters for EA Matching

A fast-paced, direct founder paired with a slow, process-heavy EA will fail — even if both are talented. PI eliminates this blind spot. We cross-reference your behavioral profile against every graduated EA to find the person whose natural operating rhythm complements yours.

The Matching Algorithm

We built a proprietary app that combines PI data with your intake answers, industry needs, schedule requirements, and communication style. AI generates initial match recommendations. Your Talent Success Manager reviews them with human context and presents your top 3+ candidates.

The Result: 93% Retention

When the behavioral match is right, everything else compounds. The EA anticipates your needs. Communication feels effortless. The relationship deepens over months, not weeks. That's why our 12-month retention is 93% — behavioral alignment is the foundation.
