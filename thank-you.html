It's a focused 30-minute conversation with Lucas, our Founder Success Lead — not a pitch. "How quickly can I actually get started?"+
If we find a match you're excited about on the call, we can have EA profiles in your inbox within 24 hours. Most founders who decide to move forward are introduced to their EA within 48 hours of their strategy call. Start dates are structured so your EA arrives ready to work from Day 1.
"What if I'm not ready to commit on the call?"+
That's completely fine. A lot of founders come to the call to learn more, not to make a same-day decision. There's no obligation until you've found an EA you're genuinely excited about. If we go through the matching process and don't find someone who's the right fit, you pay nothing. That's our Matching Guarantee.
