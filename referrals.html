Questions

Referral FAQ

When do I receive my reward?+
If you choose the free month, it's applied to your next billing cycle after your referral starts service. If you choose cash, it's sent within 30 days.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?+
No. Refer 1 person or 100. The reward is the same every time — no caps, no limits, no expiration on the program.
Can I switch between the free month and cash for different referrals?+
Yes. You choose independently for each referral. Take the free month for one, cash for the next — completely up to you.
What if I'm on an annual plan — how does the free month work?+
We credit the equivalent of one month's value toward your next renewal. At 3 referrals on the annual plan, you'd have $7,500 credited toward Year 2.
What counts as a "successful referral"?+
Your referral must sign up for a Pareto plan and start service with their right-hand. The reward is triggered once they begin their first billing cycle.
What if someone I refer was already in Pareto's pipeline?+
If they used your link or Gift Card before booking their intro call, you get credit. We track referrals by link, Gift Card, and booking form — the first referral source wins.
Do my Gift Cards expire?+
Yes, 90 days after they're issued. You receive 3 new Gift Cards every time you hit a 3-referral milestone.
I'm not a Pareto client. Can I still refer people?+
Absolutely. Sign up as a Pareto Partner — it's free. You'll earn $1,500 cash for every person you refer who starts service.
Does my right-hand really get a bonus?+
Yes. $100 for every successful referral, plus a $500 milestone bonus when you hit 3 referrals. It's real cash, paid in their next payroll cycle.
