The Rules

Your Tier Is Based on Active Referrals

  • An "active referral" is someone you referred who is currently on a paid Pareto plan and current on billing.
  • If referrals leave and your count drops below a tier threshold, you have a 30-day grace period to bring it back up.
  • If you're still below after 30 days, your ongoing rewards adjust to match your current tier. You never lose per-referral rewards already earned.
  • When you're back above the threshold, your perks resume immediately.

Think of it this way: every active referral is worth keeping. And when you truly believe in what Pareto does, keeping them is easy.

Questions

Referral FAQ

When do I receive my per-referral reward?+
If you choose the free month, it's applied to your next billing cycle after your referral starts service. If you choose cash, it's sent within 30 days.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?+
No. Refer as many as you want. The per-referral reward is the same every time, and the tier rewards keep growing.
How does the free right-hand at Gold work?+
Once you have 10 active referrals, your monthly plan charge drops to $0. You keep your right-hand, keep your Pareto OS access, keep everything — you just stop paying. This continues as long as you maintain 10 active referrals.
What if one of my referrals cancels and I drop below a tier?+
You have a 30-day grace period. During that time, your tier perks continue. If you bring your active count back above the threshold, you stay at your tier. If you're still below after 30 days, you drop to the tier that matches your current count. You keep all per-referral rewards already earned.
Can I switch between the free month and cash for different referrals?+
Yes. Each referral, you choose independently. Free month for one, cash for the next — entirely up to you.
What if I'm on an annual plan and I reach Gold?+
Your next annual renewal is covered by Pareto. If you reach Gold mid-year, the remaining months of your current term are credited back proportionally.
How are the monthly cash payments (Platinum+) handled?+
Direct deposit to your bank account, processed monthly. As long as you maintain the active referral threshold, the payment continues automatically.
Does my right-hand really get paid for each referral?+
Yes. $100 per referral, plus a $500 milestone bonus at every 3-referral mark (Bronze). By Gold tier, your right-hand has earned $2,300 in bonuses.
Do Gift Cards expire?+
Yes, 90 days after issue. You receive 3 new Gift Cards every time you hit a 3-referral milestone.
I'm not a Pareto client. Can I qualify for tiers?+
Yes. Sign up as a Pareto Partner. You'll earn $1,500 cash per referral, and your active referral count qualifies you for tiers. The monthly cash payments at Platinum+ apply to partners too. If you become a client, the Silver discount and Gold free right-hand apply.
