Prompt C — Video Transition
START FRAME: A small, dense hexagonal glass prism floating in pure darkness, glowing with compressed emerald green holographic data inside. A faint orbital ring of green light surrounds it. Minimal particles. Quiet, loaded with potential. END FRAME: Three large holographic glass panels arranged in a triangular command-center formation in the same dark void. Left panel glows green with network node patterns, center panel glows bright green with dashboard grid layouts, right panel glows teal-to-indigo with flowing data streams and knowledge graphs. Thin green connection threads arc between all three. The original prism is now a faint hollow wireframe in the center. TRANSITION: The prism hovers still for 0.5 seconds. Then it begins to pulse with intensifying light -- the internal glow brightens, the orbital ring spins faster. At the peak of intensity, the prism fractures along three clean geometric planes with a flash of white-green light. The three fragments immediately begin transforming and expanding -- each one unfolding, growing, and reshaping into one of the three large holographic panels. The expansion happens simultaneously but at slightly staggered speeds -- center panel forms first (0.2s ahead), then left and right unfurl outward. As each panel materializes, its surface content (nodes, grids, data streams) appears in a quick cascade from top to bottom, like a screen powering on. The connection threads between panels appear last, drawing themselves in thin green arcs. A burst of tiny particles releases from the center point during the fracture moment, then settles into gentle ambient drift. Camera: Completely locked. No camera movement whatsoever. The only motion is the object transforming. Dark background throughout. Style: Photorealistic 3D, glass-morphism materials, volumetric green and teal lighting. Premium, cinematic. The aesthetic of Stripe, Linear, or Apple product videos. Satisfying, precise mechanical motion with eased timing (slow-in, slow-out on all movements). Duration: 4-5 seconds total. Aspect ratio: 16:9. Quality: Ultra-high fidelity, 30fps minimum, no artifacts. Clean motion blur on expanding elements.
How to use: Upload Prompt A output as start frame, Prompt B output as end frame. Works with Runway, Kling, Pika, or Higgsfield. Try both directions -- the reverse (panels compressing into the prism) can work as a "scroll back up" animation.
